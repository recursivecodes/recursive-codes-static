In my last post, I showed you how to create a Glip team. You're probably wondering what Glip is and what you can use it for. Great question! [Glip](https://glip.com/product) is team chat - you're no doubt familiar with Slack and Rocket.Chat - it's similar to them. Team based messaging, attachments, task management, video meetings, and more. You can even build your own bots with the Glip API. I'd show you how to do that, but there's already a really [great guide](https://developers.ringcentral.com/guide/team-messaging/manual/node) to get you started creating your first bot.
